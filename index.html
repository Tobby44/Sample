Story By Tobby 44

The Legend Of Sen The Dragon

Sen, the last and most powerful dragon left roaming the skies causing chaos and destruction across the land. When all hope is lost the human race call upon their last hope, a powerful witch. Unable to destroy the dragon the witch uses the last of her power to cast a spell. Sen has not been defeated but has now been reborn as a human. How will Sen live his life now as a human who he once despised and saw as weak?

Ray is a tall young man at the start of the story, he was lean and has well defined muscles. Ray has a characteristic red hair, an influence of his past self as the Red Dragon Sen. Through each of his evolutions, his appearance aged from a teen to a young man to a man in his mid thirties. After all his task are complete he was able to revert back to his Dragon form. In his Dragon Form he looks like a red dragon with big wings, a tail and two horns on his head. His consciousness has been transfered into the two headed demon tier beast by Quinn. He is able to switch between two heads and one.

In the two headed demon tier beast. He was now a little bigger than a regular human. No longer did he have two heads and he had made his body look like that of a human. However, in doing so, using the body of the dragon, the dragon that had created them. To the people, he looked like an ancient enemy that hadn't been seen for a very long time. Ray had the appearance of a Dalki. Only covered in larger scales and had a more menacing look in its eyes looking more dragon than a man.

Ray Talen

Ray Talen is the King of Dragons and the last Dragon alive who's original name is Great Red Dragon Sen.

Quinn Talen

Quinn Talen is the Current Vampire King of the new vampire settlement, and also the Seventh and the last Vampire King of the old vampire settlement.

Jack Dem

Jack Dem is a Werewolf that is part of the Red Wings and a subordinate of Ray Talen, one of his most trusted allies and a powerful Alpha Werewolf.

Gary Bluebird

Gary Bluebird was a childhood friend of Ray's. He was a talented swordsman whose sword skills even surpassed Ray's by a wide margin.

Galen Talen

Galen Talen is the son of Quinn Talen and Layla Munrow and is an Incomplete Celestial by birth.

Chris Dem

Chris is the Agent One of Pure and one of its founders.
